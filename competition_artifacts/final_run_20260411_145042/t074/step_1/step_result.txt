Extracted finance rules from all relevant AGENTS.MD files and the finance-record-frontmatter schema.

Key rules for locating purchase bills:
1. Purchase bills live in /50_finance/purchases/ (the money lane)
2. Filename pattern: YYYY_MM_DD__eur_AAAAAA__bill__project_name.md where the leading date is the purchased_on date in YYYY_MM_DD format, the EUR amount is total_eur zero-padded to 6 digits, followed by __bill__, then an alias/project slug
3. Vendor/counterparty info is stored inside the bill file as the 'counterparty' field, NOT in the filename — must read file contents to identify the vendor
4. Bills have required frontmatter fields: record_type, bill_id, alias, purchased_on, total_eur, counterparty, project, lines
5. Line items follow ordered list with item, quantity, unit_eur, line_eur (line_eur = quantity * unit_eur)
6. Currency assumed EUR if not explicitly stated
7. Finance records are sensitive and auditable — prefer exactness over prose
8. Preserve distinction between household, business, and speculative spend
9. Do not infer authorization for financial actions from casual chat alone
10. If system docs and local context conflict, prefer the more authoritative and more specific rule
